Simple Omelette Recipe

A quick and easy dish, perfect for any meal. Customize with your choice of cheese, vegetables, or meats.

Preparation Time

  • Total: 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • 1 tbsp butter or oil
  • Optional: cheese, vegetables, meats, herbs

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs: Mix eggs with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat the pan: Add butter or oil over medium heat.
  3. Cook the omelette: Pour eggs, spread evenly.
  4. Add fillings: Once slightly set, add your fillings.
  5. Fold & serve: Cook, fold, and plate.
